Amravati Programme

Comprehensive Rural Development Initiatives in Amravati District

Jal-Jivan Mission

Implemented by the Water and Sanitation Office of the Government of Maharashtra. MGD has planned training for 4205 people from 1587 villages across 14 talukas of Amravati district.

Swachh Bharat Mission

Training, demonstrations, and reading materials provided for sewage and solid waste management. Key initiatives include plastic-free villages, waste-to-fertilizer projects, and reuse of polluted water.

Other Key Initiatives

Silt-Free Dam & Shivar

Implemented in Majri Mhasla village (Nandgaon Khandeshwar taluka). Removal of silt increased water storage and improved soil fertility.

Women's Self-Help Groups

Established production centers for economic latrines at Amravati and Tiwsa.

Tree Plantation

3-year tree plantation and enhancement program in the 40 km area between Amravati to Nagpur highway.

Media Coverage

Highlighting our impact through local news features.

अमरावती कार्यक्रम

जल-जीवन मिशन

महाराष्ट्र शासनाच्या पाणी व स्वच्छता विभागामार्फत अंमलबजावणी. MGD ने अमरावती जिल्ह्यातील १४ तालुक्यातील १५८७ गावांमधील ४२०५ लोकांच्या प्रशिक्षणाचे नियोजन केले आहे.
